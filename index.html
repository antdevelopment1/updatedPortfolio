My name is April Copes and I am a Fullstack JavaScript Software Developer. Prior to pursuing a career in software development, I was an industrial electrician for an automation conveyor installations company. There I learned how to plan, organize, and integrate critical electrical engineering upgrades to major distribution centers such as Amazon, FedEx, Walmart, and Walgreens throughout the US.

Since then, I have gained vast experience with full-stack JavaScript development, frameworks and technologies. I am open to opportunities where I can contribute my knowledge and understanding into an organization in need of skilled, dedicated and goal oriented individuals.

I have a deep desire to understand how the components of complex systems work in unison to solve challenges faced by those on a routine basis. Apart from my immense passion for technology, I genuinely am at my best when working alongside others to develop, strategize, and implement needed solutions. I seek and welcome challenge as I appreciate the rewards that come from being a part of a great team.
